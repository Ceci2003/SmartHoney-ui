Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How easy is it to install the devices?

Installation is straightforward and takes just a few minutes per hive. Our devices are designed to be non-invasive and can be placed without disturbing the bees. We provide detailed instructions and video tutorials.

What kind of data do the devices collect?

Our devices monitor temperature, humidity, hive weight, sound patterns, and bee activity. All data is transmitted wirelessly to our cloud platform for analysis and storage.

Do I need internet access at my apiary?

Yes, the devices require internet connectivity to transmit data. They support both Wi-Fi and cellular connectivity options depending on your location and needs.

Can I use SmartHoney with multiple apiaries?

Absolutely! SmartHoney supports multiple apiaries and hives. You can organize devices by apiary and compare performance across different locations.

What happens if a device fails?

If a device stops transmitting data, you'll receive an alert. We offer warranty coverage and replacement services. Our support team is available to help troubleshoot any issues.
